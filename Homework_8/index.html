About me

PROFESSIONAL PATH

In life, we are constantly growing and adapting, or we after left behind. After over a decade working in Manufacturing and Product Development in the medical industry, I found myself at an interesting inflection point. While I greatly enjoyed my career expereinces and the positive impact I've made on other's lives through my work, I knew that another 10 years of in that field wasn;t for me.

I love technology and understanding how we interact with it: from the past, to the present, and into the future. I recently graduated from the UofM Bootcamp where I developed 6 full stack web applications from mockup to deployment. Although I have the ability to develop applications from the design to server-side, my strength lies in understanding user needs, requirements and then integrating them into a finished product. I am ready to get my foot in the door and grow within a company to learn good standards and practices that will help my professional development. Ultimately, I want to grow within a company to eventually become a Senior Full Stack Web Developer.
